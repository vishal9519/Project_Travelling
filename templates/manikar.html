Manikaran

Manikaran is a picturesque town located in the northern Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. It is renowned for its hot springs and holds significant religious importance for both Hindus and Sikhs. Nestled in the Parvati Valley, Manikaran offers a serene and spiritual retreat for travelers.

Attractions:
-Manikaran Sahib Gurudwara
-Raja Maidam
-Thengal Bhawan
-Jorhat Gymkhana Club
-Hot Springs
-Kulant Pith
-Botanical Garden


Tips:
->Visit Gurudwara with Respect: When visiting the Gurudwara, cover your head, and be mindful of the religious customs.
->Local Cuisine: Don't miss trying the langar (community meal) at the Gurudwara, which is a wholesome and delicious vegetarian meal.
->Respect local customs and traditions.
->Use local transportation options wisely.

You can also drive to Manikaran, but be ready for challenging mountain roads and check the weather and road conditions before your journey.
