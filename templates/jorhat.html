Jorhat

Jorhat, often referred to as the "Tea Capital of India," is a charming city located in the northeastern state of Assam. Nestled amidst lush green landscapes and surrounded by sprawling tea estates, Jorhat holds a prominent place in India's cultural and economic tapestry. With a rich cultural heritage, a thriving tea industry, and a range of tourist attractions, Jorhat beckons visitors to explore its unique blend of tradition and natural beauty.

Attractions:
-Majuli Island
-Raja Maidam
-Thengal Bhawan
-Jorhat Gymkhana Club
-Nimati Ghat
-Matrimandir
-Botanical Garden
