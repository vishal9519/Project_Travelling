kasol

Kasol, nestled in the Parvati Valley of the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, is a picturesque hamlet renowned for its stunning landscapes and vibrant culture. Surrounded by lush greenery and the Parvati River, it has become a popular destination for backpackers seeking a tranquil escape amidst the Himalayas.
